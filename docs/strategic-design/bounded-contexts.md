# Bounded Contexts

## 1. Student Profiles
Manages the creation, storage, and retrieval of student profiles, including personal information, skills, and portfolios.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Profile creation and updates
  - Portfolio management
  - Visibility settings for profiles

## 2. Matching
Facilitates the matching process between students and recruiters based on skills, preferences, and availability.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Matching algorithm implementation
  - Notification of matches
  - Management of match-related interactions

## 3. Events
Handles the scheduling and management of recruitment events, such as job fairs and networking sessions.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Event creation and updates
  - RSVP management
  - Attendance tracking

## 4. Messaging
Manages communication between students and recruiters once a match is established.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Message sending and receiving
  - Inbox management
  - Notification of new messages

## 5. Recruiter Engagement
Manages the interactions and relationships with recruiters, including their profiles and job openings.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Recruiter profile management
  - Job opening creation and updates
  - Communication with candidates
